Projects

Search System Prototype

In Spring 2022, I took a course on UI design and user behaviors. Our final (group) project asked that we create a prototype of a "better" library service website. Our group chose to create a "friendly" search system for undergraduates when they come across indexed catalogs. The overall concept for our design -a search wizard with drop-down "term trees" that helps create a properly phrased Boolean search- was my idea, and I attempted to demonstrate it via a semi-working JustInMind prototype. The other group members took charge of the other project aspects.

The example search for our prototype was for "babies with heart defects". If you click the various fields and buttons throughout the wizard prototype, this particular search's steps will be revealed (you may need to double click some buttons to activate them). Unfortunately, we did not get a chance to integrate with a database to create a fully operational model, but the overall concepts are clear. It isn't pretty, but it works.
